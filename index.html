GEOB 479

Hi and welcome to my blog for GEOB 479, an advanced GIS course taught by Professor Brian Klinkenberg at the University of British Columbia.

I'm currently a 4th year undergraduate student studying physical geography and computer science in Vancouver, BC. Outside of school I work in the design, journalism , and non-profit spheres and am passionate about urban issues. If I’m not camping out in the GIS Lab, you’ll find me running around the beach, wandering the North Shore, or curled up with a book and a mean cup of cha ☕ .
